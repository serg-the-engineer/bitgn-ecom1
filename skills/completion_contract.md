# Skill: Completion Contract

Use this before every final answer.

Checklist:
1. Re-read the task's requested output shape.
2. Ensure the selected outcome matches the observed state.
3. For successful lookup, reporting, reconciliation, or mutation tasks, include the
   evidence references used to derive the answer.
4. For literal formats, output exactly the requested token, number, date, list, or text.
5. After a mutation, verify state before reporting success.
6. Stop after the completion tool succeeds.

Correct completion is part of the task, not a presentation detail.
